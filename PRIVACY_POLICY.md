# Ghost Check Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Ghost Check is a browser extension that helps users identify possible AI-written article content and find more credible sources. This Privacy Policy explains what information Ghost Check collects, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## Information We Collect

Ghost Check may collect and process the following information:

- Visible article text from a webpage when the extension checks an article.
- The webpage title and URL for the article being checked.
- An anonymous extension install ID used to count free checks and manage subscription access.
- Free-check usage count and subscription status.

Ghost Check does not collect passwords, authentication credentials, private messages, payment card numbers, full browsing history, precise location, or unrelated page content.

## How We Use Information

Ghost Check uses the information above to:

- Analyze whether an article may contain AI-written signals.
- Recommend more credible sources related to the article topic.
- Enforce the free-check limit.
- Manage subscription access.
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse.

## Website Content

Ghost Check reads visible website content only to perform article checks. The extension is designed to process article text when a page appears to be an article or when the user manually starts a check.

## Payments

Ghost Check uses Stripe to process subscriptions. Payment details, such as credit card numbers, are handled directly by Stripe. Ghost Check does not store full payment card information.

Stripe's privacy policy is available at:

https://stripe.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

Ghost Check does not sell user data.

Ghost Check may share limited information with service providers that are necessary to operate the extension, such as backend hosting providers, AI-analysis providers, and Stripe for subscription management.

## Data Retention

Ghost Check keeps information only as long as needed to provide the extension, enforce subscription access, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent abuse.

For local development or early testing, some usage data may be stored temporarily in memory and may reset when the backend restarts.

## Security

Ghost Check uses reasonable technical measures to protect information. However, no internet service can guarantee complete security.

## Children's Privacy

Ghost Check is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## User Choices

Users can stop Ghost Check from processing website content by not running checks or by uninstalling the extension. Users may manage or cancel subscriptions through Stripe Checkout or Stripe's customer portal when available.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the effective date above.
